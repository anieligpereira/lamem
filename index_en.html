Laboratory of Macroevolution and Molecular Evolution

About the Lab

The Laboratory of Macroevolution and Molecular Evolution investigates the processes responsible for the diversification of life through time. Our research integrates fossil data, molecular phylogenies, and comparative methods to understand patterns of morphological evolution and diversification dynamics.

Research Areas

  • Macroevolution
  • Morphological evolution
  • Phylogenomics
  • Diversification analyses
  • Fossil integration in phylogenetic analyses
